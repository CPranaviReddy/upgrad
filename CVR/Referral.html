HOW DO I REFER AN EXTERNAL APPLICANT?

An Employee Referral Form may be obtained from the Office Manager. Fill out the form or a copy of it. The applicants you refer must have this form attached to the resumes or applications that they submit to the Office Manager. You cannot refer anyone who has already applied to, who already works at or anyone who has worked here within the last twelve months. This includes regular employees, and casual employees, as well as employee working at cvr through temporary agencies.
